Activities

At Aguilar's Hotel, we offer a variety of activities for all ages. Enjoy swimming in our outdoor pool, explore the nearby hiking trails, or relax with a spa day. Our activities are designed to make your stay as enjoyable and memorable as possible.

Location

Aguilar's Hotel is located in the scenic town of Buer Aguilar, Pangasinan. Known for its natural beauty and welcoming community, Buer Aguilar offers a perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. Enjoy the serene environment and the rich culture of the local community.
