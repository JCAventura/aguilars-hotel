Our Story

Aguilar's Hotel was established in 1990 in the heart of Buer Aguilar, Pangasinan. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the premier destinations in the region, offering a blend of luxury, comfort, and warm hospitality. Our mission is to provide our guests with an unforgettable experience, whether they are here for business or leisure.

Our Vision

We strive to be the leading hotel in the region by continuously enhancing our services and facilities. We are committed to sustainability and community engagement, and we aim to contribute positively to the local community and the environment.
